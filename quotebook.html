Analog & Digital Illustrated Dadisms

I made this quote book based on the things my Father says. I used ink, dip pens, ink textures, vector artwork and digital type to put together a book that feels like a children's book, but with the true, colourful language of my Dad. I wanted it to capture his spirit of storytelling
