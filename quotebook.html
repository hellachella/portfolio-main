Typographic Quote Book

Analog & Digital Illustrated Dadisms

My Dad is a story teller and has led a unique life. I designed this book to showcase some of the sayings I grew up hearing. I wanted the book to feel like a children's book, contrasting with the sometimes rough around the edges style of language my Dad is known for.

The book uses handmade lettering and handmade ink textures along with digital type. The handmade lettering was done using a dip pen, and the ink made with paper and ink.
