Diner Branding Guide

Royal's Restaurant Branding

No matter what, order the daily sandwich.

  • Illustrator Icon

Branding guideline for a small restaurant, done as an exercise. I wrote the copy, designed the logo and branding, and created the book layout. The book is written in non jargon with short explanations around topics that I felt might need a bit more information rather than just confusing the client. This includes social media use, photography, logo use and more.
